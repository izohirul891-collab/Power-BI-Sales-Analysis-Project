Business Performance Dashboard (Power BI Project)

This project showcases a comprehensive business analytics dashboard built in Power BI, designed to track and visualize key performance indicators (KPIs) for sales and customer behavior.

Key Highlights

  • KPIs Overview: Orders, revenue, profit, cost, returns, and quantity sold with customer‑level averages.
  • Customer Analysis: Detailed table showing individual customer orders, revenue contribution, profit margins, and return rates.
  • Product Insights: Revenue and profit breakdown by product category (e.g., Road Bikes, Mountain Bikes, Tires, Helmets, Apparel).
  • Demographics: Gender and occupation‑based revenue/profit distribution.
  • Regional View: Interactive map displaying total revenue by continent and country.
  • Trends: Monthly comparisons and line charts for revenue and profit growth.

Tools & Techniques Used

  • Power BI (Data modeling, DAX, interactive visuals)
  • Excel (Data cleaning and preparation)
  • Visualization best practices for clear storytelling

Outcome

The dashboard provides actionable insights into sales performance, customer demographics, and product profitability. It helps decision‑makers identify top‑performing categories, monitor return rates, and evaluate regional revenue distribution.
